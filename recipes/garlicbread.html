Garlic Bread

Description

Lomo Saltado is a Peruvian stir fry dish that's easy to make with few steps and ingredients. It can also be made with chicken (pollo saltado), but in this recipe, we're using beef (lomo)

Ingredients

  • - 16 ounces of frozen French fries
  • - 1 pound beef (flank steak, skirt steak), sliced about 1/4" thin
  • - Half a clove of garlic, minced
  • - 1 medium red onion, sliced
  • - 3 large tomatoes, peeled, sliced, and seeded
  • - 1 yellow chili pepper (aji amarillo chili or paste if possible)
  • - Oil, for cooking
  • - Soy sauce
  • - Salt
  • - 2 tablespoons chopped freshed parsley (for topping)
    •

    Steps

    1. FRENCH FRIES PREP: Following the instructions for your package of french fries, prepare them so that they're light golden, usually about 25 minutes
    2. BEEF PREP: In a separate bowl, marinate the sliced beef with 1 tablespoon of soy sauce and half of the garlic. Set aside for at least 15 minutes.
    3. COOKING: In a frying pan or wok, add about 2 tablespoons of oil over medium-high heat. Add in the marinated beef and cook until about medium-rare, then remove beef from the pan and set aside. Add a bit more oil if necessary, then add the onions and cook until slightly translucent. Stir in the tomatoes, chili, and remaining garlic. Add a tablespoon of soy sauce. Once tomatoes are a bit softened, add the beef back in with cooked french fries. Stir and fry for about another 2-3 minutes, or until beef at desired wellness. Season with salt to taste. Turn off the heat and set aside.
    4. SERVING: On an individual plate, add a bowl or scoop of rice with a side of the lomo saltado. Top the lomo saltado with chopped parsley. Enjoy.
