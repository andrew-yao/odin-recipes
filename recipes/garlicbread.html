Garlic Bread

Description

It's garlic bread. Who doesn't love this?

Ingredients

  • - 3 heads garlic
  • - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • - 1 (1 pound) loaf italian bread
  • - 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • - 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
    •

    Steps

    1. GARLIC PREP: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 C). On a sheet of aluminum foil, slice the tops off garlic heads and drizzle with olive oil. Wrap up and bake until garlic is soft, about 30 minutes.
    2. GARLIC SPREAD PREP: Squeeze garlic cloves into a bowl. Stir in butter, cheese (if adding), and parsley.
    3. BAKING: Set the oven on broil. Slice the bread into individual pieces and apply garlic spread. Broil until toasted, about 5 minutes.
