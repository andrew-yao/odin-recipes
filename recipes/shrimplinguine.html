Linguine with Shrimp and Lemon Oil

Description

A light, refreshing pasta dish that's perfect for the summer weather or a cozy night in. Flavors are light and fresh which makes this an always great choice.

Ingredients

Lemon Oil:

  • - 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • - 1 lemon, zested
    •

    Pasta:

  • - 1 pound linguine pasta
  • - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • - 2 shallots, diced
  • - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • - 16 ounces frozen shrimp
  • - 1/4 cup lemon juice (about 2 lemons)
  • - 1 lemon, zested
  • - 1 teaspoon salt
  • - 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • - 3 ounces arugula (about 3 packed cups)
  • - 1/4 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
    •

    Steps

    1. LEMON OIL: Combine the olive oil and lemon zest in a small bowl. Set aside.
    2. PASTA: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high heat. Add the pasta and cook until tender but still firm to the bite. Stir occasionally and let cook for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain pasta but reserve 1 cup of the cooking liquid.
    3. COOKING: In a large skillet, warm the olive oil over medium heat. Add shallots and garlic, cooking for 2 minutes. Add shrimp and cook until pink, about 5 minutes. Add cooked linguine, lemon juice, zest, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine. Turn off the heat and add the arugula.
    4. SERVING: Using a mesh sieve, strain the lemon zest out of the reserved lemon olive oil and add the oil to the pasta. Add cooking water to desired consistency. Add chopped parsley to pasta, toss to combine, serve immediately.
