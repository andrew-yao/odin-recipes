Lomo Saltado

Description

Lomo Saltado is a Peruvian stirfry dish that's easy to make with few steps and ingredients. It can also be made with chicken (pollo saltado), but in this recipe, we're using beef (lomo)

Ingredients

  • - 16 ounces of frozen French fries
  • - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • - 1 pound beef trip tip, sliced to 1/8" - 1/4" thickness
  • - 1 large onion, sliced
  • - 3 large tomatoes, peeled and sliced
  • - 1 yellow chili pepper (aji amarillo if possible)
  • - 1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
  • - Soy sauce (add to taste)
  • - Salt (add to taste)
  • - 2 tablespoons chopped freshed parsley (for topping)
